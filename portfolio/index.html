Zalando Help Center Case Study

Although I mostly worked within their Mobile Apps team, I had the opportunity to embed in a different (and neglected) team at Zalando: Customer Care who enjoyed very little R&D support, as well as being locked into old vendor contracts. The project was to redesign the Help Center experience. The case study itself highlights the Product and Design development process I typically follow. What started with the idea of lower costs for Customer Care ended up becoming a cornerstone project for the team and their future tech strategy.
